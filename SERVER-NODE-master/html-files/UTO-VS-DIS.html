INTERNET, UTOPIA or DISTOPIA?


UTOPIA: οὐ «not» e τόπος «place», it literally means "A place that doesn't exist"
It represents an ideal place where everything is perfect. 		DISTOPIA: δυσ "bad" e τόπος «place», it literally means "The worst place".
Dystopias are characterized by fear or distress, tyrannical governments,
environmental disaster, or other cataclysmas that decline in society.


"Whether it is to be utopia or dystopia will be a touch-and-go race right up no the final moment.... Buckminster Fuller"

Tim Berners-Lee, who founds the World Wide Web (WWW), is creating a campaigne to delete the Fake News form Internet.
"The humanity connected to the technology works in a distopian way" by Tim Berners-Lee himself.


The PARADOX of the INTERNET

It should create a sort of Cyberdemocracy, thanks to the big amount of devices or civic and government technologies.
It is a form of government in which all adult citizens are presumed to be eligible to participate equally in the proposal, development and creation of laws.

Big Companies struggle to control and regulate information traffic (Amazon, Google, Apple)
